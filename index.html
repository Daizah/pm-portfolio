About Me

I don't just manage projects.
I build the tools too.

I'm a PMP-certified Project Manager and MBA with 5+ years in B2B telecom sales, specializing in enterprise account management, cross-functional execution, and client onboarding. I don't just manage projects. I build the tools that make them run.

At T-Mobile for Business, I managed 35+ corporate B2B accounts across mobility and connectivity products, exceeded revenue targets by 112–130%, and built a coded pricing tracker with plan-eligibility logic that was adopted org-wide. I also served as Interim Sales Manager, building the onboarding curriculum, KPI dashboards, and sales process documentation for an 11-person new hire class.

At Verizon Business Group, I manage an SMB and enterprise portfolio positioning fiber, SD-WAN, and connectivity solutions. Through 48in48, I've led nonprofit website launches in 48-hour Agile sprints with 100% on-time delivery.
